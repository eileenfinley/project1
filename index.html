Ultimate Frisbee

What is Ultimate Frisbee?

A niche sport

Ultimate frisbee is a sport that many don't know about. Many confuse it with disc golf, a sport that imitates golf. Ultimate frisbee is a team sport where teams face off on a field. It is 7 versus 7 with endzones at each end of the field. There's lots of strategy and cardio involved in the sport.

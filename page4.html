Ultimate at Truman

What does Ultimate look like at Truman?
Women's Ultimate Men's Ultimate Intramurals
The Women's Ultimate team is called TSUnami! They practice twice a week. They have about 3-4 tournaments per semester and travel all around the country. They compete competitively against many different schools. The Men's Ultimate team is called JuJiTSU! They also practice twice a week. They are very similar to the women's team with having 3-4 tournaments per semester across the country. Both teams are club teams at Truman. Intramural frisbee is only local to Truman's campus. Typically, it is less experienced players. They play a game a week with no practices and play against other teams on campus. Intramural frisbee is much more casaul on campus than club frisbee.
